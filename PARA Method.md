
A simple system to organize your entire digital life into four categories.

## The Inbox

This is the most powerful feature of a note-taking system.

Low-friction capture, organize later.

## The 4 Categories

**Projects** — Short-term efforts with a clear goal and deadline.
*Examples: "Build a portfolio website," "Plan trip to Japan"*

**Areas** — Ongoing responsibilities you maintain over time.
*Examples: Health, Finances, Career, Home maintenance*

**Resources** — Topics of interest you collect for future reference.
*Examples: Recipes, Kotlin, Photography tips*

**Archive** — Completed projects and inactive items you may need later.

## How to Choose

The rule is simple: every note goes into one of these four folders. 

If you're not sure, ask yourself — *"Is this something I'm actively working on?"* 

If yes, it's a Project. If it's an ongoing responsibility, it's an Area. 

If it's just interesting, it's a Resource. 

If it's done, archive it.
